FAQ

Good to know

Is Days Since private?

Yes. There are no accounts, no ads and no tracking. Your data stays on your device and in your own private iCloud — there's no developer server for it to be sent to.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up and no login. Open the app and start tracking.

Does it sync across my devices?

Yes, through your private iCloud. Your iPhone and iPad stay in sync automatically, with no account needed.

Is there a subscription?

No. Days Since is free to start, and a single one-time Pro unlock removes the limit. Pay once, yours forever.

What's the difference between events and reminders?

Events count up — the days since you last did something, like a chore or a habit. Reminders count down — the days until an upcoming date, like a birthday or anniversary.

Can it import birthdays from my calendar?

Yes, optionally. Days Since can read your calendar when you choose to import, so you can bring in birthdays and recurring dates in a couple of taps. Calendar access is used only for import.
